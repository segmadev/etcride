## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Active User Accounts
- **Account information:** Retained while account is active, deleted upon account deletion request (after admin approval)
- **Trip data:** Retained for 1 year (for dispute resolution and analytics)
- **Location data:** Retained for 90 days (or longer if disputes are active)
- **Payment records:** Retained for 3 years (tax and regulatory compliance)

### 6.2 Deleted Accounts
- **Personal data (name, email, phone):** Permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion approval
- **Transaction history:** Retained indefinitely for audit and legal compliance
- **Trip records:** Retained for 5 years (regulatory and tax requirements)
- **Support messages:** Retained for 1 year unless part of ongoing disputes

### 6.3 Backup & Archives
- Deleted data may persist in backup systems for up to 90 days
- Archived data is encrypted and stored securely
- Archive access requires admin authorization

### 6.4 Device-Level Biometric Data
- Biometric data (fingerprint, face) is processed only on device
- No biometric data is transmitted to or stored on ETCRide servers
- Device is the sole repository of biometric data

## APPENDIX: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Is my location tracked when I'm not using the app?
**A:** Only if you grant location permission "Always." We recommend "Only While Using App" for privacy. You can change this in device settings.

### Q: Can I opt out of all data collection?
**A:** Some data collection is essential for service (location, payments, identity). Optional data (marketing, analytics) can be disabled.

### Q: Who can see my personal information?
**A:** Only the driver (during trip), support staff (if you contact them), and admins. Payment processors see only necessary payment info.

### Q: How long is my data kept after I delete my account?
**A:** Personal data is deleted within 30 days. Transaction history is retained for 5 years for legal compliance.

### Q: Can I request a copy of my data?
**A:** Yes. Contact support and request a data export. You'll receive your data in a standard format within 30 days.

### Q: Is my biometric data stored on ETCRide servers?
**A:** No. Biometric data (fingerprint, face) is processed only on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

### Q: What happens if there's a data breach?
**A:** We'll investigate immediately and notify affected users within 72 hours. We'll also cooperate with authorities if required.

### Q: How do I request deletion of my account?
**A:** Go to Settings → Delete Account → Enter reason → Confirm. Admin reviews within 24-48 hours.
